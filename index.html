Stock Market 101 — The Need-to-Know

What is it?

  • Stock = tiny slice of a company.
  • ETF = basket of many stocks (less guesswork).
  • Index = benchmark list (S&P 500, Nasdaq-100).
  • Fee (expense ratio): VOO/VTI ≈ 0.03% / yr.
  • Dividends can auto-reinvest (DRIP).

Real Talk — The Fears vs. The Facts

“It’s too complicated.”

You don’t need to pick single stocks. Start with one core ETF (VOO or VTI). 15 minutes to open, 5 minutes to buy.

“What if I lose money?”

Short-term swings happen. Historically, diversified US markets trend up over long horizons. Automate and let time work.

“I’m late at 53.”

Not late. 10–20 years is meaningful compounding time—especially with steady monthly deposits.
